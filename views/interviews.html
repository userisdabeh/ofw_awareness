Across Borders, Beyond Sacrifice: The Journey of an OFW

Tyrone Maningas

Singapore

When Maningas first left the Philippines to work for a better future abroad, he didn’t bring with him just his luggage and other personal belongings — he also brought with him the weight of his family’s hopes and future. With limited opportunities at home and the promise of better pay overseas, he took the opportunity despite the uncertainties. “My main motivation was providing a better future for my family — education for my children, medical care for my parents, and financial stability,” he shared.
